WHAT IS MOTORSPORT

Aerodynamics

Advanced bodywork and winglets optimize airflow, enhancing stability and top speed on straights and corners.

Telemetry

Real-time data collection provides crucial insights into performance metrics, helping teams fine-tune the bike's setup.

Execution

Riders must flawlessly apply their skills, managing speed, braking, and cornering under immense pressure during the race.

QUALIFYING

MotoGP's qualifying system features two 15-minute sessions (Q1 and Q2), where the fastest riders from practice and Q1 determine the starting grid, with Q2 deciding the top 12 positions.
